About OnTime Moving & Storage

Your trusted moving partner for over 22 years, serving the Lower Mainland with excellence and dedication

Our Story

Since our founding over two decades ago, OnTime Moving & Storage has been committed to making moving stress-free for families and businesses throughout the Lower Mainland. What started as a small local moving company has grown into one of the most trusted names in the industry, thanks to our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and professional service.

We've successfully completed over 11,000 moves, each one treated with the same care and attention as if we were moving our own belongings. Our A+ BBB rating and hundreds of 5-star reviews reflect our dedication to excellence in every aspect of our service.
